          Data transparency

          Methods before metrics

          Every indicator is defined conservatively to minimize overstatement and protect editorial and reviewer confidentiality.

          01

          Publication and usage

          Publication indicators include records marked Published in OJS. Article views and PDF file requests are reported as separate COUNTER-ready usage events.

          02

          Web of Science

          Citation links are summed across verified JSOMER DOI records. Citing documents are deduplicated by WoS accession number.

          03

          Scopus

          Citation links are summed from Scopus Cited by counts. Citing documents are reported as unique Scopus records. Database-specific counts are not merged.

          04

          Peer review

          Review statistics are aggregate assignment indicators. Declined, cancelled, incomplete, and unconsidered assignments are excluded from timeliness measures.

        Data transparency

        How these indicators are calculated

        OJS publication and usage data

        Publication indicators include records marked Published in the supplied OJS Articles Report. Rejected, active, copyediting, and production submissions are excluded. Article views count OJS usage records classified as Article; PDF totals count PDF file requests linked to published content.

        Country data are derived from published-author affiliations. International collaboration means that a published article includes authors affiliated with more than one country.

        WoS and peer-review data

        Web of Science citation links are summed across verified JSOMER DOI records. Citing documents are deduplicated by WoS accession number; one document may cite multiple JSOMER articles.

        Peer-review figures are aggregate assignment statistics. Reviewer names, email addresses, recommendations, comments, manuscript-level links, and individual completion times are not published.

        Counts are periodically updated and may change as OJS and Web of Science records develop. Corrections may be sent to editor@jsomer.org.

      Coverage and sources

      This dashboard includes records marked Published in the supplied OJS Articles Report and usage events in the supplied PKP COUNTER-ready report. Rejected, active, copyediting, and production submissions are excluded.

      Article views count OJS records classified as Article. PDF totals count file requests linked to published article titles and identified as PDF files.

      Interpretation

      Usage data may include legitimate repeat visits and downloads. Statistics are intended to describe access and engagement, not scholarly quality, research validity, or citation impact.
